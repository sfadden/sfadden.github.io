Steve Fadden

Lecturer, UC Berkeley School of Information

About Me

Steve Fadden is a lecturer at UC Berkeley, with research interests including research methods, Human Computer Interaction, User Experience, and Cognitive Psychology. He has extensive experience in technology research and design across industry, academia, and consulting.

His research focuses on understanding how people use technology, make decisions, learn, and accomplish their work.

Research Interests

  • Research Methods
  • Human-Computer Interaction (HCI)
  • User Experience (UX)
  • Cognitive Psychology
